### First day in the South
Waking up is hard this morning; the accumulation of stages is taking its toll. Nevertheless, we set off at quarter to five for a long climb, whose summit reveals a magnificent sunrise.

Today is an easy stage — sun and rolling terrain on the programme. We walk quickly through the grassy hills. The mist, however, does not lift from the mountains.

### San Petru di Verdi
The San Petru relay is our stop for the night; we arrive at 1pm. Fat beef ribs crackle over a wood fire, hunters are seated at the tables. We settle into this cheerful atmosphere, and our travelling companions quickly join us.
The afternoon is gentle; we drink a few pastis in good company, around a map to plan tomorrow's stage.
Unfortunately, a big storm is forecast and nobody seems ready to face it in their tent.

With alternative plans ready, everyone heads to bed, ready to face what promises to be a gruelling day.
