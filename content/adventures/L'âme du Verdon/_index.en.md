As the cool spring air still blows over the Alpes-Maritimes, my friend Guillaume and I set out
on a multi-day initiatory journey, in search of the soul of the Verdon. The seasonal cold and rain will not dampen our
pleasure
in discovering these sometimes desert-like landscapes, following in the footsteps of Jean Giono.
