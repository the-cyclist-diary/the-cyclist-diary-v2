### Little Climb and Chat
Up early again, after a very restful night, we set off for this final stage with a clear goal: to reach Cahors.
On the first slope that takes us to the top of the causse, we meet a group of pilgrims on the Camino de Santiago and chat with them a bit. Eventually we leave them behind and descend back to the banks of the Lot.

### Long way home
Unfortunately, the rest of the route is quite long and monotonous. Along the Lot, in a small muddy woodland, we drag our sore feet, with no other view than poor cornfields.
The arrival in Cahors is nonetheless joyful. A bit of music in town, a small pizzeria that seemed to be waiting for us, welcomes us.

Just enough time to enjoy a good lunch, see the famous bridge of the city, and we're already at the station, ready to head back to Bordeaux, but above all, ready for adventure!
