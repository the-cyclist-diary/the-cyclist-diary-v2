The _Picos de Europa_, or _Peaks of Europe_, are part of the Cantabrian Mountain Range, straddling the provinces of Asturias, León and Cantabria. This Spanish massif, less famous than its neighbouring range across the border, is nonetheless home to a trek that has become legendary: the Ring of the Peaks.
Through the rocky summits and rolling green hills, we hope to complete the entire route in a week... But is that even possible?
